PROMPT: UGC CTA Variant Generator
===================================

Generate 6 different CTAs for a UGC ad.

Each CTA must:
- Feel natural and human, not like a traditional ad
- Be 1–2 sentences max
- Vary in tone

Include exactly one of each type:
1. Urgency-based
2. Soft-sell
3. Social proof
4. Low-risk
5. Direct
6. Community-driven

Product: [PRODUCT NAME]
Platform: [PLATFORM]
Price: [PRICE]

1. URGENCY: Grab it before they restock — link in bio.
